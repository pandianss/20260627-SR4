Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026. Data controller: Satish Pandian S.

SuperRecall JAIIB & CAIIB ("the app", "we", "us") explains in this policy what we collect, why, and your choices. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by IIBF, RBI, SEBI, IRDAI, or any regulator.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers: When you sign in, we use Firebase Authentication. A stable anonymous user ID is created on first launch. If you create an account, we process your email address (email sign-in), Google account basic profile (Google sign-in), or phone number (phone OTP), as applicable.
  • Learning data: Your study activity—lessons viewed, flashcard recall ratings, spaced-repetition schedule, and mock-exam answers and scores—to power your reviews and progress.
  • Diagnostics: Basic crash and error information to keep the app stable.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

2. How We Use It

  • To provide the core features (spaced repetition, mocks, progress, sync).
  • To back up your progress and sync it across your devices.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.

3. Where It is Stored

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) under your user ID. Google processes it as our service provider; see Google's privacy policy. Your learning data is access-restricted so that only your authenticated account can read or write it.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • We keep your data while your account exists.
  • You can delete your account at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete account). This permanently removes your account and all associated data (learning progress and identifiers) from our systems.
  • To request deletion without the app, contact us at sspandian.here@gmail.com.

5. Children

The app is intended for working banking professionals and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction).

6. Permissions

The app uses network access to sync your data and open official regulator links. It does not access your contacts, location, camera, or photos.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
