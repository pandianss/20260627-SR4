SuperRecall Banker ("the app", "we", "us") is a study app for candidates
preparing for Indian Institute of Banking & Finance (IIBF) certifications. This
policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. **We are not affiliated
with or endorsed by IIBF, RBI, SEBI, IRDAI, or any regulator.**

## Information we collect

- **Account identifiers.** When you sign in we use Firebase Authentication. A
  stable anonymous user ID is created on first launch. If you create an account,
  we process your **email address** (email sign-in), **Google account basic
  profile** (Google sign-in), or **phone number** (phone OTP), as applicable.
- **Learning data.** Your study activity — lessons viewed, flashcard recall
  ratings, spaced-repetition schedule, and mock-exam answers and scores — to
  power your reviews and progress.
- **Diagnostics.** Basic crash and error information to keep the app stable.

We do **not** sell your data, and we do **not** use it for advertising.

## How we use it

- To provide the core features (spaced repetition, mocks, progress, sync).
- To back up your progress and sync it across your devices.
- To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.

## Where it's stored and who processes it

Data is stored in **Google Firebase** (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) under
your user ID. Google processes it as our service provider; see Google's privacy
policy. Your learning data is access-restricted so that only your authenticated
account can read or write it.

## Data retention and deletion

- We keep your data while your account exists.
- **You can delete your account at any time** from within the app
  (Account → Delete account). This permanently removes your account and all
  associated data (learning progress and identifiers) from our systems.
- To request deletion without the app, contact us at **sspandian.here@gmail.com**.

## Children

The app is intended for working banking professionals and is **not directed to
children under 13** (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction).

## Permissions

The app uses network access to sync your data and open official regulator links.
It does not access your contacts, location, camera, or photos.
